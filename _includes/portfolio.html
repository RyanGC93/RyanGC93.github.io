Three.js/Gulp/Sass/Jekyll

Welcome to my online portfolio, a blend of technology and creativity showcasing my work with stunning dynamism. Using Three.js, Gulp, Sass, and Jekyll, this site is a pinnacle of modern web design and development efficiency.

Three.js enhances the portfolio with interactive 3D experiences, bringing projects to life through engaging animations and detailed models. Gulp automates crucial tasks like script minification and image optimization, streamlining updates and maintenance. Sass revolutionizes CSS management with its robust features, enabling intricate yet clean styling. Finally, Jekyll provides the backbone, offering simple content management and templating ideal for frequent updates, all while ensuring easy deployment via GitHub Pages for seamless access.
