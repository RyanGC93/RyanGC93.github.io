Code The Dream

Code the Dream is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing free coding education and career opportunities in technology to people from diverse and underrepresented backgrounds. Through its comprehensive programs, participants learn to build software and develop apps that make a real-world impact. The initiative not only equips individuals with essential programming skills but also helps them launch careers in the tech industry, fostering inclusivity and empowerment.

  • Mentor and support diverse aspiring developers through their coding bootcamp, covering fundamental programming concepts in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more.
  • Lead coding workshops and group projects to enhance teamwork and problem-solving skills, collaborating with Code the Dream staff to ensure curriculum relevance and alignment with industry standards.
  • Advocate for diversity and inclusion in the tech industry, serving as a role model and inspiring students to leverage their unique backgrounds and perspectives.
